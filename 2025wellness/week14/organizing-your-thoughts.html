🧠 Organizing Your Thoughts to Speak Clearly

Get your ideas in order before you speak – be clear, confident, and calm!

🔍 Why It Matters

When your thoughts are organized, your words come out better! It helps you:

  • ✔️ Speak clearly
  • ✔️ Stay on topic
  • ✔️ Avoid saying “uhhh…” too much
  • ✔️ Feel confident when sharing your ideas

🪄 Magic Formula: 3-Part Talk

1. Start strong (Introduction) 🎤
Say what you're going to talk about.

2. Say your points (Middle) 🧱
Give 2–3 short points or examples.

3. Wrap it up (Conclusion) 🎁
Say why it matters or what you think.

🎯 Quick Talk Planner

Use this when you need to speak on the spot:

  • 💡 Main Idea: What’s your point?
  • 🎯 One Reason: Why do you believe it?
  • 🗣️ Example: Can you give a real-life example?
  • ✅ Final Thought: End with a conclusion.

🧘‍♀️ Calm the Mind Before You Speak

Do this if your mind feels jumbled before talking:

  • 🧘‍♂️ Breathe in for 4... hold... out for 4
  • 📄 Jot down 3 words you want to say
  • 👂 Say it out loud to yourself first
