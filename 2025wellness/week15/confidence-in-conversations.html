🚀 What Does Confidence Look Like?

Check ✔️ the things that show confidence in a conversation:

  • ☐ Making eye contact 👀
  • ☐ Speaking too fast 🏃‍♀️💨
  • ☐ Smiling 😊
  • ☐ Saying “um” a lot 🤔
  • ☐ Asking questions 🗣️❓
  • ☐ Looking at the floor 😶‍🌫️
