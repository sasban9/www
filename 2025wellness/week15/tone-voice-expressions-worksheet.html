🧠 Warm-Up: What’s the Difference?

Match the term with the correct definition:

  • 1️⃣ Tone - ______
  • 2️⃣ Voice - ______
  • 3️⃣ Expression - ______

Definitions:

  1. A. The actual sound and pitch used to speak 🎶
  2. B. The feelings and attitude shown through your words 💬
  3. C. Facial gestures, posture, and eye contact when speaking 😄👀
