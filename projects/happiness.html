🌱 Simple Pleasures

Watching a sunrise or sunset Eating your favorite meal mindfully Receiving a heartfelt compliment Going for a walk in nature Listening to your favorite music on a quiet night

Learning a new skill or hobby (e.g., painting, chess) Taking a solo day trip to a nearby town Camping under the stars Completing a physical challenge (e.g., 5K run, hiking a mountain) Building something with your hands (e.g., a table, a garden)
