Module 1 — Think With AI

Objective: Learn to generate ideas, make decisions, and break problems using AI.

Session Duration: 60 Minutes

Session Structure

  • 0–5 min: Hook + Demo
  • 5–15 min: Idea Generation
  • 15–30 min: Decision Making
  • 30–45 min: Problem Breakdown
  • 45–55 min: Mini Project
  • 55–60 min: Recap

Hook Demo

Prompt:

Give me 30 ways a student in India can earn ₹10,000/month online.

Follow-up:

From this list, pick top 5 ideas based on:
- low investment
- fast to start
- high demand
Explain why

Insight: Think faster using AI

Skill 1 — Idea Generation

Problem: Blank page thinking

AI removes overthinking

Idea Generation Workflow

Give me 30 business ideas for:
[students / professionals / homemakers]
Categorize these ideas into:
- Easy
- Medium
- Advanced
From these, pick 3 ideas that:
- can start within 7 days
- require less than ₹5,000

Exercise

Generate 20 ideas for yourself

Shortlist 2 ideas

Insight: Expands thinking space

Skill 2 — Decision Making

Problem: Too many options

AI provides structured comparison

Decision Example

Compare:
- Freelancing
- YouTube
- Digital products

Evaluate based on:
- time to earn
- effort
- scalability
- risk

Recommend best option

Follow-up Prompt

What is the biggest mistake beginners make when choosing this path?

Exercise

Decide:

  • Career path
  • Skill to learn
  • Business idea

Insight: Clarity through comparison

Skill 3 — Problem Breakdown

Problem: Goals feel too big

AI breaks into steps

Problem Breakdown Example

Break down this goal:
Earning ₹50,000/month online

Create a step-by-step 30-day plan
What should I do in the first 7 days?

Exercise

Take one personal goal

Break into actionable steps

Insight: Converts ambition into execution

Mini Project

Design your AI thinking system

Step 1 — Idea

Give me 20 ways to earn money online based on my skills:
[enter skills]

Step 2 — Decision

From these ideas, suggest best one based on:
- beginner friendly
- fast income

Step 3 — Plan

Create a 14-day action plan to start this

Expected Output

  • 1 idea
  • 1 decision
  • 1 execution plan

Recap

  • AI = Thinking partner
  • Ideas → Decisions → Execution
  • Prompts = thinking tools

Closing

If you can think better, you can earn better.
