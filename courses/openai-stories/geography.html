Continents

Asia

Asia is the largest continent, covering about 30% of the Earth's land area. It is home to over 4.5 billion people, more than 60% of the world's population.

Africa

Africa is known for its vast deserts, rainforests, and diverse wildlife. It is the second-largest continent and the birthplace of humanity.

North America

North America consists of three large countries: Canada, the United States, and Mexico. The continent features diverse landscapes, from the Arctic to the tropics.

Countries and Their Facts

Brazil

Brazil is the largest country in South America, known for the Amazon Rainforest, the world's largest tropical rainforest.

Australia

Australia is both a country and a continent. It is the only continent that is also a country, known for its unique wildlife like kangaroos and koalas.

India

India is the seventh-largest country by land area and the second-most populous country. It has a rich cultural and historical heritage.

About Geography

Geography is the study of places and the relationships between people and their environments. It helps us understand the world's physical features, human societies, and how they interact with the environment.
