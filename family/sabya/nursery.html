English oral
1) Do you know Alligator Ann!
2) Come to school just in a time .
3) Recognition of alphabets with objects A To J ( Expect Letter G)
Written
1) Trace letters A To J ( Expect Letter G).
2) Match Letters with objects A To J ( Expect Letter G).
3) Circle Letters A , B , C and D.
4) Match similar letters A to J.

1) Ten little fingers Ten little toes.
