🎶 Music & Emotion

Have you ever listened to a song and instantly felt happier, sadder, braver, or calmer? Music is powerful because it connects directly to our emotions. 💓

“Music is what feelings sound like.” — Unknown

It can express what words sometimes can’t. Certain songs mirror what we’re feeling, helping us understand ourselves better.
