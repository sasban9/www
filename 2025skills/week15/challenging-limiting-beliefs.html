🌫️ What Are Limiting Beliefs?

Limiting beliefs are thoughts that hold you back. They often sound like:

  • ❌ “I’m not smart enough.”
  • ❌ “I always mess things up.”
  • ❌ “People won’t like the real me.”
“You have power over your mind — not outside events. Realize this, and you will find strength.” — Marcus Aurelius

These beliefs are usually not facts — just old stories we’ve been telling ourselves.

🛠️ Challenge the Belief

Pick one belief and break it down:

  1. 🧠 What is the belief?
  2. 🔎 What evidence do I have that supports it?
  3. 📉 What evidence proves it wrong?
  4. 💪 What is a new, empowering belief I can try instead?
